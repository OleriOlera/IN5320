Projects:

Locus

Locus is a mobile-application inspired by Goal Management Training(GMT). It is designed to help users keep track of their own goals, and to help structure their work. The goal of the design is to translate the structure of GMT to a mobile interface to provide the opportunity for self administering of the techniques.

Goal Management training

Goal Management Training(GMT) is a form of cognitive behavioral therapy designed to help patients with forms of Excecutive Dysfunctions.
